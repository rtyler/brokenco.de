R\. Tyler Croy has been an open source developer for over 20 years. Originally
getting his start in the [FreeBSD](http://freebsd.org) project, and then
working with the [Python](https://python.org),
[Ruby](https://ruby-lang.org/en/), [Puppet](https://puppet.com), and [Delta
Lake](https://delta.io) communities. 

Most of his formative open source work has been within the [Jenkins
project](https://jenkins.io), where he helped with advocacy, governance, and
community infrastructure. 


## Non-profit

Tyler was a board member for [Software in the Public
Interest](https://spi-inc.org), the Jenkins project, and helped initiate the
[Continuous Delivery Foundation](https://cd.foundation).

Currently Tyler is treasurer for the [Blue Tailed Skinks](https://bluetailedskinks.com), a local cycling non-profit.


## Hacking

Recently his focus has been in the [Delta Lake](https://github.com/delta-io)
and [Rust](https://rust-lang.org) communities, exploring the potential
use-cases for Rust in high concurrency data processing for customers of [Buoyant Data](https://buoyantdata.com).
